# Rep-Seq project 


### Objectives in a nutshell:
Analyze Rep-Seq/AIRR-seq results and identify are 16 T-cell samples from 2 patients (8 samples from each):
- CD4/CD8
- memory/native
- From the 1st or 2nd patient
+ whether the patient is immune to CMV
